Banking & Financial Services (BFS)

Accelerating the cashless economy efficiently

…by adopting global standards & local priorities

The Banking and Financial Services (BFS) Group at NeST Digital has established itself with over two decades of experience as a pioneer and leader in the GCC market by providing advanced payment platform solutions. Our offerings are based on scalable microarchitecture and modern technology stacks, implemented across 30+ leading financial institutions – a true testimony to our established credentials in this domain.

Key Capabilities

  • End-to-end payment platform solutions for banks and financial institutions
  • Support for card payments, wallets, UPI, QR, POS and internet / mobile banking
  • Scalable microservices-based architecture ready for high transaction volumes
  • Integration with global payment networks and regional switches
  • Compliance with international standards and local regulatory guidelines

Our BFS Offerings

  • Digital payment ecosystems (Retail & Corporate)
  • Core banking integration & middleware development
  • Fraud detection, AML, and risk monitoring support systems
  • Customer onboarding, KYC and e-KYC workflow solutions
  • Migration and modernization of legacy banking applications

What We Help Banks Achieve

  • Accelerate the move towards a cashless economy
  • Deliver seamless, secure and user-friendly digital payment experiences
  • Optimize operational costs with modern, modular architectures
  • Meet regulatory and compliance requirements with confidence
  • Launch new products faster in highly competitive markets

Why NeST Digital for BFS?

  • 20+ years of proven presence in the GCC financial technology space
  • Deep understanding of both global standards and local priorities
  • Strong partnership track record with major banks and financial institutions
  • Combination of domain expertise, technology capability and delivery excellence
